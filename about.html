Who are we?

We are a group of people who focus our advocacy on men's issues. We understand that men face unique challenges in today's society, and we strive to create a platform where their concerns can be heard and addressed.

What do we advocate for?

Our mission is to promote gender equality by highlighting the specific obstacles and injustices that men encounter, fostering a supportive environment for open discussions, and advocating for policies that promote fairness and well-being for everyone

Mental Health

We recognize that, while societal expectations and cultural norms keep men from seeking help, we also wish to critique the system's inability to adress and help sove men's issues properly.

And while many men often may feel discouraged by various factors from seeking help, many others do actively seek help but the system isn't equipped to help them properly.

This can lead to higher rates of suicide, substance abuse, and untreated mental health conditions. Through education, advocacy and destigmatization, we aim to create a supportive environment where men's issues are heard and people take a step forwards into adressing them properly with empathy and care.

Fatherhood

We strive to address the issue of fatherhood and family rights. It is essential to recognize the significance of fathers' roles in children's lives and advocate for policies that ensure equal parenting rights and responsibilities.
